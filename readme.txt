== Description ==

Display your XBOX 360 Gamertag on your sidebar.
You need to have a [My MGC](http://www.mygamercard.net/) account

== Installation ==

1. Upload `xboxgamertag.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Place the widget and enter your gamertag in the widget settings.
